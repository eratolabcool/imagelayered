## Introduction

Welcome to **Qwen Image Layered AI**, an AI-powered image editing service that transforms how you work with visual content. At Qwen Image Layered AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information and creative content. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our AI image layering, editing, and related services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with exceptional AI-powered image editing experiences:

### 1. Account Information
- **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, and optional profile information when you create an account.
- **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and communicate about service updates.

### 2. Images and Visual Content
- **What We Collect**: Images you upload for processing, including image metadata (EXIF data, dimensions, file format).
- **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered layer decomposition, recoloring, object replacement, and other editing services.
- **Storage**: Images are temporarily stored during processing and may be retained based on your subscription plan.

### 3. Usage and Interaction Data
- **What We Collect**: Information about how you use our services, including:
  - Features accessed (layer decomposition, recoloring, object removal, etc.)
  - Number of images processed
  - Credits consumed
  - Processing parameters and settings
- **Purpose**: To improve our AI models, optimize service performance, and develop new features.

### 4. AI Processing Data
- **What We Collect**: Processing results, layer data, and editing history associated with your account.
- **Purpose**: To provide the service, enable re-editing, and improve AI model accuracy.

### 5. Payment and Billing Information
- **What We Collect**: Payment method details, billing address, transaction history, and credit usage records.
- **Purpose**: To process payments, manage subscriptions, and provide billing support.
- **Security**: Payment information is processed securely through third-party payment providers (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) and is not stored on our servers.

### 6. Device and Technical Information
- **What We Collect**: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and browser fingerprint.
- **Purpose**: To optimize service performance, ensure compatibility, and prevent fraud.

### 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
- **What We Collect**: Cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies.
- **Purpose**: To enhance functionality, personalize your experience, and analyze usage patterns.

## How We Use Your Images

### Image Processing
- Your uploaded images are processed using our AI models to decompose layers, recolor elements, replace objects, and perform other editing operations.
- Processing occurs on secure servers with encrypted transmission.

### Temporary vs. Persistent Storage
- **Temporary Storage**: Images are stored temporarily during processing (typically 24-72 hours) and then automatically deleted.
- **Persistent Storage**: Based on your subscription plan, processed images and layers may be stored for up to 30-90 days. You can delete your images at any time.
- **Downloaded Content**: Once you download edited images, they are stored on your device and are not subject to our retention policies.

### AI Training
- **Opt-out Available**: We do NOT use your images to train our AI models without your explicit consent.
- **Anonymized Data**: We may use anonymized, aggregated processing data to improve our algorithms, but this cannot be linked back to your specific images or account.

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
- **Regular Audits**: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Compliance**: We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations

### Data Retention
- **Free Accounts**: Images and processing data are deleted after 30 days of inactivity
- **Paid Subscriptions**: Images and processing data are retained according to your plan (60-90 days)
- **Account Deletion**: Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information or images. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

### 1. Service Providers
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe, PayPal, and other payment providers to process transactions
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS, Google Cloud, or other cloud services to host our services
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Plausible, or similar tools (with your consent)

### 2. Legal Requirements
- To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- To prevent fraud or illegal activity

### 3. Business Transfers
- In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new owner

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 1. Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Download your images and processing history

### 2. Deletion
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Delete specific images or processing history

### 3. Correction
- Update or correct your account information
- Modify processing preferences

### 4. Opt-Out
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Opt-out of analytics tracking
