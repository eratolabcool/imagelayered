## Technical Highlights

### AI-Powered Layer Decomposition
We've trained specialized deep learning models that understand the semantic content of images, automatically identifying and separating people, objects, backgrounds, and other elements. What used to take hours of manual work now takes seconds.

### Intelligent Color Transfer
Our recoloring feature doesn't just change colors—it maintains consistency in lighting, shadows, and textures, ensuring edited images look natural and realistic.

### Context-Aware Object Replacement
When replacing objects in images, our AI understands the scene's contextual relationships, automatically adjusting lighting, perspective, and scale to perfectly integrate new elements into existing scenes.
